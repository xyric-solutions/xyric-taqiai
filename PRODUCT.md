# TaqiAI — Product Context

## Product Purpose

TaqiAI is Pakistan's first AI-powered legal platform for practicing lawyers. It drafts court documents, researches case law, and provides AI legal guidance — all grounded in Pakistani law (PPC, CrPC, CPC, SCMR).

## Register

product

## Users

**Primary:** Pakistani advocates (lawyers) — active court practitioners in Lahore, Islamabad, Karachi, and other bar councils. Typically 30–55 years old, fluent in both Urdu and English, work in chambers or small firms. Comfortable with smartphones but cautious about new software. Value speed, accuracy, and professional credibility above all.

**Secondary:** Law students and junior advocates using TaqiAI for research and learning.

**Key insight:** These users are time-poor and trust-sensitive. They will abandon a tool immediately if it produces an embarrassing legal error. Every interaction must feel authoritative.

## Brand Tone

- **Authoritative** — like a senior advocate, not a startup chatbot
- **Precise** — legal professionals notice vague language immediately
- **Warm but professional** — Pakistani hospitality without informality
- **Bilingual naturally** — Urdu and English coexist as equals, neither feels secondary

Tagline: "Your Legal Mind, Amplified" / "آپ کا قانونی ذہن، مضبوط تر"

## Anti-References (Do NOT look like these)

- **Harvey.ai** — cold blue, enterprise-gated, American, requires demo request
- **DigiLawyer** — outdated government-portal aesthetic, low trust
- **LegalZoom** — consumer-focused, American, no Pakistani law
- **Generic SaaS dashboards** — cookie-cutter admin templates
- Any tool that looks like it was built for a Western market and rebranded for Pakistan

## What It Should Feel Like

"A premium leather-bound law journal — dark, structured, authoritative — but with the speed and intelligence of a modern AI terminal."

Linear (for speed) × Notion (for content clarity) × a Lahore High Court chamber (for authority)

## Key Differentiators (reflect in UI)

1. **Pakistan-specific** — PPC, CrPC, CPC, SCMR citations built-in
2. **Bilingual** — Urdu + English, Nastaliq font, RTL support
3. **Transparent pricing** — PKR pricing, visible on landing page
4. **Self-serve** — no "Request a Demo" gate, direct sign-up
5. **170+ templates** across 12 Pakistani practice areas
6. **AI trust signals** — every AI output is labeled "AI Generated — verify before filing"

## Practice Areas

Criminal Law, Family Law, Property Law, Civil Law, Corporate Law, Constitutional Law, Tax Law, Immigration, Affidavits, Agreements, Applications, Non-Muslim Laws

## Core Pages

## Emotional Design Goals

| When user does this | They should feel |
|---|---|
| Opens the dashboard | In control, informed, powerful |
| Starts AI drafting | Like they have a senior colleague |
| Reads an AI output | Confident it's grounded in Pakistani law |
| Searches case law | Like using a research tool, not a search engine |
| Sees Urdu content | At home, not like a translation afterthought |
