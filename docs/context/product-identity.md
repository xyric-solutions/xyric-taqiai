## Product Definition

TaqiAI is an **AI-powered legal practice platform** purpose-built for the **Pakistani legal system**. It combines legal reasoning AI (Google Gemini) with a searchable corpus of Pakistani court judgments and statutes to help lawyers draft court-ready documents, research precedents, manage cases, and handle day-to-day practice — all in one place. It is **live and in daily use**, not a concept.

**Primary capability: Legal Drafting** — Domain expert validation (REC-091, REC-092) confirms that drafting court documents (plaints, written statements, bail applications) is the highest-value use case. A practicing lawyer assessed pre-existing generic AI drafting as "very poor" and unusable for court proceedings, establishing both the market gap and the quality bar. The platform has since grown beyond drafting into a full suite — research, advisory, case management and document tooling — that surrounds and strengthens the drafting workflow.

**The live platform delivers 13 modules:** AI Document Drafting (50+ templates, 13 legal categories), Court-Case Drafting, Tax Calculator, AI Legal Advisor, Judgment Intelligence search, Chamber / Case Management, Legal Translation (Urdu/English/Arabic), Lawyer Diary, Case Builder (judgment-backed drafting), Copy from Photo (OCR), Voice Case (discussion → draft), Statute Search, and the Document Vault. See [`../epics/MASTER-EPIC.md`](../epics/MASTER-EPIC.md) and [`pillars.md`](./pillars.md).

## Mission Statement

> "To democratize access to intelligent legal analysis for Pakistani lawyers, enabling both plaintiff and defendant counsel to draft stronger, more thorough legal documents through AI that understands Pakistan's unique legal framework — built on British common law, Islamic jurisprudence, and local statutory authority."

## Vision Statement

> "By 2028, TaqiAI will be the trusted AI legal assistant for Pakistani lawyers — the tool they rely on daily for case analysis, argument construction, and document drafting across civil and criminal practice, setting the standard for jurisdiction-specific legal AI in emerging markets."

## Core Value Proposition

**"Your Legal Mind, Amplified"**

We transform case facts into structured legal arguments grounded in Pakistani law — covering both plaintiff and defendant perspectives with citations to PPC, CrPC, CPC, and relevant statutory authority.

## Key Differentiators

| Differentiator | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Pakistan-First Jurisdiction** | Built specifically for PPC, CrPC, CPC, and Pakistani statutory law — not a US/UK tool adapted |
| **Bidirectional Capability** | Both Forward (facts → draft) and Reverse (judgment → analysis) modes |
| **Plaintiff AND Defendant** | Equal capability for both sides — no prosecution/defense bias |
| **Civil + Criminal Coverage** | Unified tool across practice areas, not siloed by case type |
| **Accuracy-First Development** | Internal testing with solved cases before any productization |
| **Multi-Agent Pipeline** | Research → Reasoning → Drafting → Verification chain for reliable output |

## Unique Benefits

1. **Jurisdiction-Specific Intelligence** — Pakistani law citations, not generic legal reasoning
2. **Dual-Perspective Analysis** — See both sides of any case before court
3. **Solved-Case Validated** — Every capability tested against known-good outcomes
4. **Structural Compliance** — Documents follow Pakistani court formatting requirements
5. **Time Savings** — Hours of legal research compressed into minutes
6. **Reduced Hallucination** — Multi-stage verification pipeline catches errors

## Strategic Principles

1. **Accuracy Over Speed** — Better to be right slowly than wrong quickly
2. **Internal-First** — Prove capability with solved cases before selling
3. **Jurisdiction Depth Over Breadth** — Master Pakistan before expanding
4. **Both Sides of the Bar** — Never bias toward prosecution or defense
5. **Human-in-the-Loop** — AI assists, lawyers decide — always
