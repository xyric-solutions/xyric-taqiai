# TaqiAI - User Personas

> **Source:** Product Vision v1.0

---

## Primary Personas (Phase 1 Focus)

### P1: The Civil Litigation Lawyer (40% of users)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Experience** | 5-20 years |
| **Profile** | Handles property disputes, contract cases, family law, tort claims |
| **Behavior** | Drafts written statements, plaints, and appeals regularly; relies on precedent research |
| **Pain Points** | Hours spent on precedent research, inconsistent argument structure, manual citation checking |
| **Key Laws** | CPC (Code of Civil Procedure 1908), Contract Act 1872, Transfer of Property Act 1882, Family Courts Act 1964 |

**Success Story:**
> "TaqiAI helped me identify three relevant High Court precedents I'd missed for a property dispute case. The draft written statement was 80% usable with minor adjustments."

---

### P2: The Criminal Defense Lawyer (30% of users)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Experience** | 3-15 years |
| **Profile** | Handles bail applications, defense arguments, appeals against conviction |
| **Behavior** | Works under time pressure especially for bail; needs quick access to defenses and precedents |
| **Pain Points** | Urgent bail applications with limited prep time, finding defense precedents, arguing against prosecution evidence |
| **Key Laws** | PPC (Pakistan Penal Code 1860), CrPC (Code of Criminal Procedure 1898), Qanun-e-Shahadat Order 1984 |

**Success Story:**
> "I had 3 hours to prepare a bail application for a Section 302 case. TaqiAI analyzed the FIR, identified procedural weaknesses, and drafted arguments citing relevant Supreme Court bail jurisprudence."

---

### P3: The Prosecution/Plaintiff Criminal Lawyer (20% of users)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Experience** | 5-25 years |
| **Profile** | Handles criminal complaints, charge sheets, prosecution arguments |
| **Behavior** | Builds cases from FIRs and evidence, drafts complaints and prosecution arguments |
| **Pain Points** | Ensuring charges match evidence strength, covering all relevant PPC sections, anticipating defense arguments |
| **Key Laws** | PPC, CrPC, Anti-Terrorism Act 1997, Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act 2016 |

**Success Story:**
> "TaqiAI's reverse analysis of a similar solved case showed me exactly how the prosecution built their argument chain. I adapted the same structure for my case."

## Secondary Personas (Future Phases)

### P4: The Paralegal/Junior Associate (Future)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Profile** | Assists senior lawyers with research, drafting, and document preparation |
| **Need** | Efficient first-draft generation, precedent summaries, citation verification |

### P5: The Law Student (Future)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Profile** | Studying for bar exams or legal clinics |
| **Need** | Understanding case analysis methodology, learning argument construction |

### P6: The Judge/Judicial Officer (Future — Read-Only)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Profile** | Uses AI for case law research and judgment drafting assistance |
| **Need** | Comprehensive case summaries, relevant precedent compilation |
