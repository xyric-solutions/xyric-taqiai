# [M-001] Product Discovery Complete

## Summary
Established TaqiAI as an AI-powered legal drafting and analysis assistant for the Pakistani legal system. Completed market research, brainstorming, vision document, product scaffolding, and Phase 1 Claude skills for internal testing.

## Decisions Made

| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Pakistan-first jurisdiction focus | 200,000+ lawyers with zero AI tools; US/UK saturated |
| Bidirectional workflow (Forward + Reverse) | Tests AI from both angles; analysis informs drafting |
| Internal skills first, no app | Prove accuracy before productization; reduce risk |
| Civil + Criminal coverage | Maximizes addressable market; most lawyers do both |
| Plaintiff AND defendant capability | No bias; serves entire profession |
| Accuracy-first development | Legal hallucination is malpractice risk; must beat 17-33% baseline |
| Category 14-legal for skills | 09 already taken by client-services; next available number |

## Context

TaqiAI is the first product in Xyric's portfolio targeting the legal vertical. Pakistan's legal system (British common law + Islamic law hybrid) requires jurisdiction-specific AI — no global tool addresses this. The internal-first testing approach aligns with Xyric's accuracy-over-speed principle.
