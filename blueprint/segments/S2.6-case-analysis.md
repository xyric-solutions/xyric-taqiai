## Why this is one buildable unit
Case Analysis (Reverse Mode) is one unit: the builder page plus the analysis and prepare APIs take raw matter input and produce a structured decomposition that hands off a `draftRequest` to the drafting engine — verifiable in one pass (paste a matter → get parties/facts/issues/law/missing-info → prepared draftRequest).
